Quandles and Knot Colorings

Undergraduate Senior Project

Quandles, Knot Theory, and Sharac(n)

My senior project focused on quandles and their relationship to knot theory. I studied how quandle operations reflect the Reidemeister moves and how quandle homomorphisms can represent valid colorings of knots.

I also investigated a proposed family of finite algebraic structures called Sharac(n), including possible axioms, examples, counterexamples, and relationships between their cycle structures.
